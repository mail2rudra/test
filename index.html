मेरो बर्ष

म स्वस्थ - मेरो देश स्वस्थ

Let this year be a life-long commitment to be healthy, and contribute in building a healthy and prosperous Nepal.

मेरो प्रतिबद्धता

I will refrain from consuming alcohol and tobacco products.

I will be physically active and exercise regularly.

I will eat healthy and
nutritious food.

I will schedule
regular health check-ups.

I will be attentive towards the health and well-being of my family and community.
